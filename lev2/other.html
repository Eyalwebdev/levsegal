About Lev Segal

Born in Ukraine in 1933, immigrated to Israel in 1973.
Studies: Graduate of the art institute in Kiev.
Segal was a member of the artists’ village Sha-Nur.
Since 1986 he has been exhibiting his works
privately and as part of group exhibitions in:
Moscow, Kiev, France, Germany, United States and Israel
